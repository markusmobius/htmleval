36 Hours+
 Independent short breaks inspired by The New York Times’s travel feature. Each four-day, three-night itinerary includes set activities, like a tour or cooking class, as well as optional activities and suggestions based on the original “36 Hours in …” itinerary from The Times. These journeys are intended for travelers who don’t have time for a long vacation, or who want to extend a Times Journey. Whether it’s an intimate getaway, a family trip or one for a group of your choosing, “36 Hours+” provides inside knowledge based on the expertise of The New York Times.
