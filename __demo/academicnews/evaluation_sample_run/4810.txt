Mall Tenants Play Hardball In Lease Negotiations
Mall tenants are asking for more concessions from landlords facing pressure to fill their properties.
Retailers, food and beverage operators and department store chains looking to renew their leases have been asking for more allowances from mall owners for renovations, lower rents or more flexible terms, said landlords, brokers and real estate lawyers.
