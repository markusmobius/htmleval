Quake Pushes Shares Lower
Updated Oct. 22, 1999 2:21 a.m. ET 
 
TAIPEI, Taiwan -- Stocks fell Friday for the fifth consecutive session after selling in technology shares was sparked by an early Friday earthquake .
The Taiwan Stock Exchange Weighted-Price Index fell 95.27, or 1.2%, to 7559.63. Declining issues led advancers 371 to 110, with 55 unchanged. Volume was worth 72.38 billion New Taiwan dollars.
