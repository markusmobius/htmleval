'Woke' grandma embodies spirit of Women's March

Take Elda McQuade. Marching in New York, she carried a protest sign saying, "Woke AF Grandma," using a slang term for awareness. "I'm marching for truth, equality, justice and kindness," she told CNN. Women and men, boys and girls, took to the streets Saturday to protest issues ranging from birth control access to immigrants' rights.
