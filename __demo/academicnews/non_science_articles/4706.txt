Gold Reached for Records, As Dollar Stabilized: What Does it Mean?
Nov 19, 2009 11:54 am ET 
 
Gold’s move lower today comes on a bit of dollar strength. But as Camilla Sutton, currency strategist at Scotia Capital pointed out in a quick chat with MarketBeat, gold’s series of record highs it has recently notched has come as the dollar stabilized a bit. In other words, it wasn’t a short-term spurt of dollar weakness that spurred dollar’s gains lately. What does that tell us? “It really just speaks that it is a gold story not anything else but gold,” said Sutton, which suggests that the market sentiment towards the yellow metal is growing ever more bullish.
