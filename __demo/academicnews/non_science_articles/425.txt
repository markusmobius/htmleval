CNN Profiles - Mike Galanos - HLN Host - CNN
Mike Galanos
HLN Host
HLN host, Mike Galanos, covers the human side of the news and taps into stories that matter most in neighborhoods across America. His passion and experience is reflected in his reporting. Mike first joined HLN in spring 2002, and played a key role in the network's coverage of the first anniversary of the September 11th attacks. Prior to HLN, he covered sports for CNN and CNNSI.
