What Did The 'Mad Men' Premiere Tell Us About How The Show May End?
Don Draper and company returned to AMC for one last time on Sunday before the series comes to a close in May. The show is known for slowly building momentum for a late-season climax, so what does the premiere tell us about what creator Matthew Weiner is setting up for the final episodes? HuffPost Live's Ricky Camilleri analyzed the episode with a panel of TV critics during Monday's episode of "Spoiler Alert," which also broke down the latest on "American Crime" and what's coming up on Netflix's "Daredevil."
