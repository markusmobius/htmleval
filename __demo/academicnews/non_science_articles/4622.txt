S&P Lowers Ratings, Outlook for Several Private Equity-Backed Energy Companies
Oct 5, 2015 6:09 pm ET 
 

Several private equity-backed oil and gas companies received credit-ratings downgrades or outlook revisions by Standard & Poor’s Ratings Services on Monday. The ratings actions were a result of S&P’s lowering of its oil- and gas-price assumptions last week, the credit ratings firm said. S&P uses the price assumptions in its credit ratings modeling for exploration and production companies.
