Corrections and clarifications: The original story misidentified the gender of Donal Logue's child.
Gotham star Donal Logue's teenage daughter has gone missing, the actor said on Twitter Tuesday.
Logue's 16-year-old daughter Jade went missing in Brooklyn on Monday, the actor wrote.
“Missing - yesterday at 2PM Barclays Center/Fort Greene Brooklyn: my child, Jade Logue. 6’2″ 180 lime green hoodie dark green military parka,” Donal tweeted to his 99,900 followers.
