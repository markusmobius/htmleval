Chairman of PwC’s US Board and Managing Partner of PwC’s Southern California, Arizona & Nevada Market
Brian is the lead partner for the Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences, including the annual balloting for the Academy Awards. He has been a part of the leadership balloting team since 2014. In addition to his role on the Oscars®, Brian serves as PwC’s US Board Chairman and is a member of the firm's Global Board. He is also the managing partner for PwC’s Southern California practice. Brian has more than 30 years experience providing technical accounting, auditing and business advisory services to some of PwC's largest and most complex multinational clients.
