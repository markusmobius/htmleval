Overall, do you have a favorable or unfavorable impression of Donald Trump?
This Washington Post-ABC News poll by telephone June 8-12, 2016 among a random national sample of 1,000 adults, including users of both conventional and cellular phones. The results from the full survey have a margin of sampling error of plus or minus 3.5 percentage points. Sampling, data collection and tabulation by SSRS of Media, Pa.
