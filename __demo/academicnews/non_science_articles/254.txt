Show Description

Erica Hill
Host, On The Story with Erica Hill
Veteran morning TV anchor and award-winning journalist Erica Hill rejoined HLN in July 2016, where she anchors On the Story with Erica Hill, a daily, New York-based daytime news program, launched in October. Hill also contributes to CNN as both an anchor and correspondent.
