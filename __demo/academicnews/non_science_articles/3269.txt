Girls aren't the only ones that want to have fun. The self-proclaimed risque traveler is seeking adventurous times in hot domestic vacation spots like Las Vegas and Hawaii, as well as popular international destinations like Cancun, Amsterdam, and Antigua, according to Tripology, a division of USA TODAY Travel. With an average budget of $7,438 these travelers are primarily interested in spa, culinary tours/food & wine, and self-guided tour activities. Of all the traveler types, the risque traveler is willing to spend the most on their vacation itineraries.
