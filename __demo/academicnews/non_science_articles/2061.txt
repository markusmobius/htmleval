Stephanie Rosenbloom is a staff columnist for The New York Times, where she writes the Getaway column for the Travel section, as well as features about solo travel and notable destinations.
Over the years she has had beats in the Business, Styles, Real Estate and Metro sections, chronicling American social trends about technology, money and happiness, and love in a digital age. She covered breaking news for the Continuous News desk, as well as for the Business section, where she reported on the ups and downs of the retailing industry during the financial crisis of 2008.
She is a featured writer in “The New York Times, 36 Hours: 150 Weekends in the USA & Canada” (Taschen, 2011) and “The New York Times Practical Guide to Practically Everything” (St. Martin’s Press, 2006).
Ms. Rosenbloom has been a guest on national television and radio programs on CNN, NBC, WNYC and many others.
Her personal website is StephanieRosenbloom.com.
