Aleppo's Mohamad traded schoolbooks for mechanic work
Mohamad, 11, left school in the second grade. Thaer Mohammad

 Mohamad Jabas misses going to school: he left in the second grade amidst airstrikes. Now 11, he spends his time working as a mechanic to help support his family. 
 "I came to work so I can feed my parents and earn money," he explained.
