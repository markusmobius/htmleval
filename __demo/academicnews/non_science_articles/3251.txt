Barcelona is most-requested destination in Spain
Thinking about a holiday in Spain? Barcelona was the most requested Spanish destination this week according to Tripology, a division of USA TODAY Travel. With an average budget of $2,800, travelers are looking to Barcelona for romantic getaways. From honeymoons to babymoons, travelers are interested in culinary tours/food & wine and self-guided tour activities. Travel agents say consumers are looking now to book their Barcelona trip for this coming December.
