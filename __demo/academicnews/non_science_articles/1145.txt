Princess-India is currently an intern with HuffPost's trending news team. As a senior at the Medill School of Journalism she has ran the school's only black-interest publication, BlackBoard magazine, and in the past has worked with In These Times, the National Association of Black Journalists and the National Lesbian and Gay Journalists Association.
