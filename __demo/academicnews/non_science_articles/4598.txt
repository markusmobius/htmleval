Dillard's to Sell 26 Mercantile Stores
In Separate Deals to Proffitt's, May
Updated Aug. 3, 1998 11:07 a.m. ET 
 
ST. LOUIS -- Department-store operator Dillard's Inc. has agreed to sell 26 stores owned by Mercantile Stores Co. in separate transactions to Proffitt's Inc. and May Department Stores Co.
Terms were not disclosed.
