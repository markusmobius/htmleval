Debra A. Smith is a writer and attorney specializing in Administrative Law, who resides in Baltimore Maryland. Debra was a contributor to the anthology, More of Life Spices, Seasoned Sistahs Keepin' It Real, and she has been published in the Baltimore Sun. Debra is a member of the Black Writer's Guild of Maryland and the Maryland Writer's Association. Debra Can be reached at debwrite47@yahoo.com.
