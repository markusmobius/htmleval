Technology Will Speed You Through the Airport of the Future
July 15, 2015 11:38 a.m. ET 
 
Like a good maître d’, the airport of the future will recognize you, greet you by name and know exactly where to put you.
Airports around the world are beginning to move in this direction. At London’s Gatwick Airport, beacons identify you by your smartphone and give GPS-like directions to your gate, pointing out food or shopping along the way.
