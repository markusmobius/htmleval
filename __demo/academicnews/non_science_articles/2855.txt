Walk-up songs — like the ones used by Major League players — should absolutely be a part of golf.
Wouldn’t you love to hear what your favorite golfer would swagger up to the first tee to? I know I would, especially if said golfer would bob his or her head to it, or even show off a dance move or two.
I’m bringing this up because it turns out Tiger Woods actually has an answer to the question of what his walk-up song would be. He was asked in a recent rapid-fire Q&A and he answered right away:
