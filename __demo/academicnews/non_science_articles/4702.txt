Ohio State Judge Is Expected
To Rule on Guns Litigation
Updated Oct. 7, 1999 12:25 a.m. ET 
 
A state-court judge in Cincinnati is expected to issue an important early ruling today in the nationwide litigation against the gun industry.
One person involved in the Cincinnati case said late yesterday that the court had informed at least some of the participants in the case that the judge would dismiss that city's lawsuit against the industry.
