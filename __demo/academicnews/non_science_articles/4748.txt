College Endowments Rose 12.2% in Fiscal 2017, Reversing Decline
Jan. 25, 2018 12:01 a.m. ET 
 
College endowments grew by more than 12% last year, thanks to a global stock market rally and private equity gains. 
But long-term returns aren’t as buoyant, and the amount of money schools are pulling from their endowment funds continues to grow faster than inflation. Adding to finance chiefs’ worries, some of the nation’s colleges and universities are bracing for a new tax on their endowments.
