Birthdays, bachelor parties big in Vegas
With all there is to do and see in Las Vegas, it's no wonder Vegas continues to be the most requested destination city by travelers according to Tripology, a division of USA TODAY Travel. With an average budget of $3,279, travelers appear to be willing to spend more this week on their Vegas vacation. If you are going to Vegas to celebrate a birthday, you are not alone. As the most requested reason to hit the Strip, birthday revelers are willing to spend more to celebrate in style with an average budget of $4,208. Bachelor/Bachelorette party celebrations top the spending budget this week with an average budget of $4,583.
