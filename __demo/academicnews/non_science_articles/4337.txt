Do you approve or disapprove of the way the U.S. Congress is doing its job?
This Washington Post-ABC News poll was conducted by telephone Jan. 20-23, 2014, among a random national sample of 1,003 adults, including landline and cell phone-only respondents. The margin of sampling error for overall results is 3.5 percentage points. Sampling, data collection and tabulation by Abt-SRBI of New York.
