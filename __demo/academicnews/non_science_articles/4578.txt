Metals: Copper Gains on U.S. Growth Optimism
Outlook boosts industrial metal; gold rises 
 Dec. 20, 2017 6:12 p.m. ET 
 
Copper prices rose Wednesday, boosted by optimism on growth in the U.S. and around the world.
Copper for March delivery closed up 1.4%, at $3.1960 a pound, on the Comex division of the New York Mercantile Exchange, the highest close since late October.
