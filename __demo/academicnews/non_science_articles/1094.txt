A non-partisan organization dedicated to involving Illinois residents in the state's political process
Reboot Illinois is a nonpartisan, independent website and social media effort dedicated to involving Illinoisans in the key issues facing our state, including state debt, corruption and waste and improving business and schools. Reboot Illinois, owned by AFK Media Group, covers the ideas, people and politics behind state government. Editorial content is directed by Editor Matt Dietrich, previously with the Illinois State Journal-Register, and Publisher Madeleine Doubek, formerly the executive editor of the Daily Herald.
