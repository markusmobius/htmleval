COMPANY NEWS; EQUITY RESIDENTIAL IN DEAL WITH LINCOLN PROPERTY
Equity Residential Properties Trust, the nation's largest apartment owner, said yesterday that it had agreed to acquire 25 multifamily properties in four states from the Lincoln Property Company for $465 million. Equity will pay cash and operating partnership units for the properties, which have 5,774 units. The agreement also includes assumed debt. Equity Residential is a Chicago-based real estate investment trust controlled by the financier Sam Zell. Late last year, Equity Residential and Lincoln, based in Dallas, formed a venture to develop $1 billion of apartment properties over three years.
