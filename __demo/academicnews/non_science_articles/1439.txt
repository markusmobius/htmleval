How to Watch the Meet the Press Film Festival with AFI
Share
 The Meet the Press Film Festival in Collaboration with the American Film Institute featured 16 short political documentaries produced by filmmakers from across the country. The inaugural film festival was held in Washington, D.C. on November 13th. A showcase of the films was available for a limited time to watch online and on demand. 
 Want to watch the Meet the Press Film Festival with AFI but can’t make it to Washington, D.C.? Here’s how you can catch the short documentaries for yourself. 
 Online 
 If you’re watching on a computer, you can watch the films on NBC News Digital until November 27th, 2017.
