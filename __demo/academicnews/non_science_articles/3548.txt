Newark Liberty International Airport Guide
Contact information and flight status
panynj.gov/airports/newark-liberty.html; 973-961-6000 (24 hours).
Checkpoint savvy
Busiest times: Terminals A, C and Terminal B-1 checkpoint: 5:30 a.m. - 8 a.m. and 2 p.m. - 6 p.m. Terminal B-2: 2 p.m. - 8 p.m. Terminal B-3: 2 p.m. - 8 p.m.
Depending on your airline and your status with TSA, expedited TSA PreCheck lanes may be available.
Parking
Most convenient: Valet Parking at Garage P4, $40 up to 24 hours, $20 for each additional 12-hour period. Short-term parking (Lots A & B, and garage C), $39/day, $4/each half-hour. See more options.
Least expensive: On-airport Daily lots: Lot P1/P3, $27/day; Garage 4, $34/day. Economy long-term parking: Lot P6, $18 for first 24 hours, $6 for each additional 8 hours; shuttle bus to terminals. See more options.
Cellphone lot: EWR does not have a cellphone lot.
Transportation to/from city
Most convenient: Taxi (not including tolls/tips/surcharges), $15-18 to Newark, $20-$25 to City of Elizabeth, $50-$70 to New York City. Travel time varies. Note: $5 surcharge added during weekday rush hours to New York, plus surcharge for using credit card. Tips and tolls not included. See fares and more options.
Least expensive: AirTrain Newark connects to NJTransit, PATH and Amtrak. NJTransit to New York Penn Station, about $12.50, including airport rail station access fee. Travel time: 30 minutes. Public bus service is available through NJ Transit Bus Lines. See fares, routes and schedules. Newark Airport Express bus to NY Port Authority terminal, Grand Central and Bryant Park, $16/$28 RT (plus $1 admin fee). Travel time: About 40 minutes. See more options.
Wi-Fi and charging stations
Complimentary 30-minute Wi-Fi sessions are available in all terminals. Additional fee-based Wi-Fi service is via Boingo. Unlimited Wi-Fi browsing available at Terminal C (United) on OTG-supplied iPads at gates and in restaurants.
Power outlets: Complimentary power charging stations are located in all terminals, post-security.
Go local
Good choice for local gifts: Most news and gift shops, including America! (Terminals A and B) carry souvenirs. Metropolitan Museum of Art (C120-139). See more options.
Good choice for local food: Terminal A: Phillips Seafood. Terminal B: Garden State Diner. Terminal C: Daily (Gate C72; farm-to-terminal fare, with menu that changes daily); Caps Beer Garden (Gate C130; 48 domestic/international beers and an Asian American-inspired menu), Wanderlust Burger Bar (Gate C96), Happy Clam (Gate C106), Saison (Gate C120, French cuisine). See more options.
Tips and tidbits
Newark Liberty has nursing stations in Terminal A (by Gates 30-39) and in the Terminal B3 Hallway, indoor and outdoor pet relief stations, and a medical office (Building 339). See more services and amenities by terminal.
Nearby layover option
Jersey Gardens Outlet Mall has restaurants, a 20-screen movie theater and an arcade. A shuttle bus ($9 round trip) is available from the P4 stop on the AirTrain.
