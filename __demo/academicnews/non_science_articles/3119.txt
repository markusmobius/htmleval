Predicting the NCAA tournament field in November is always a challenge.
College basketball forecasts are based on key returnees, incoming talent and, of course, coaching. But seeding these teams as the selection committee does in March takes the guesswork to another level.
Because the 2017-18 tips off Friday, here's a look at the projected field of 68. March Madness? Here's November Madness:
► No. 1 seeds: Duke, Arizona, Michigan State, Kansas
► Last four in: VCU, Vanderbilt, Georgia Tech, Oklahoma
► First Four out: Auburn, Arkansas, Georgia, Stanford
