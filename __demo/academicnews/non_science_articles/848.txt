Dustin Fitzharris is a graduate of Columbia University’s Graduate School of Journalism. His work has appeared in various publications across the country. Fitzharris specializes in entertainment/celebrity stories and over the years has interviewed some of the biggest names in the industry. In 2008 he was named a “Rookie of the Year” reporter by the New York Press Association. Fitzharris is based in New York and works as an Associate Producer at ABC News. He previously worked as the Associate Producer of "All My Children."
