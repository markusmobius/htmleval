Enquirer's Accused Podcast Nominated for Webby Award
CONNECTTWEETLINKEDINEMAILMORE
Cincinnati Enquirer's popular podcast, Accused, has been nominated for a Webby Award for Best Writing in the Product & Digital Audio Category. The Webby Awards recognize excellence on the Internet including Websites, interactive advertising and online film and video.
Launched late last summer, Accused, quickly became popular and was soon at the top of the charts in iTunes.
