Jack Anderson is a writer, producer, director, attorney and founder of The Farm, a New York-based creative/production company which specializes in storytelling, branding, and design. The Farm works with a wide variety of clients, ranging from Fortune 100 companies to startups. Jack writes for The Huffington Post and livinghealthy.com on health and wellness issues, and he is a nationally recognized speaker. His book, "Stand by Her: A Breast Cancer Guide for Men" was selected by The Wall Street Journal as a "Best Health Book of The Year." He recently updated the book to help all caregivers, entitled "Stand By Her: A Breast Cancer Guide For Loved Ones." He has appeared on TODAY, CNN and ESPN, and has written for a wide array of publications, including The New York Times, Rolling Stone, and MIT Technology Review. Jack is a licensed attorney in New York and Virginia, specializing in corporate, entertainment, and trademark law.
