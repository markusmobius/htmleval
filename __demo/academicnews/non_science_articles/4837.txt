Meritor Consolidates Units, Names New Chiefs
Updated Nov. 12, 2012 12:00 p.m. ET 
 
 Meritor Inc., an auto and truck parts maker, said it reorganized into two business segments instead of three to improve operating efficiency and named executives to oversee the groups. 
The Troy, Mich.-based company will report future results under two segments: commercial truck and industrial, and aftermarket and trailer. The move combines its previously separate commercial truck and industrial groups.
