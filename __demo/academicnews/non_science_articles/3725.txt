Hotels offer Black Friday, Cyber Monday sales
Black Friday has arrived and Cyber Monday is just days away—and hotels are offering deals that will make traveling cheaper into next year.
Many hotels throughout the USA are offering deals starting this Friday, the day after Thanksgiving, into Cyber Monday, Nov. 27. They range from discounts to credits to added perks—or all of the above.
Here is a sampling of notable sales:
Deep discounts
Tracelers heading to Walt Disney World can nab 50% off best available rates at The Grove Resort & Spa Orlando from Black Friday to Cyber Monday. The offer is valid for stays through Sept. 30, 2018. The hotel has one, two and three-bedroom suites with full kitchens, living rooms and dining rooms from $149 a night. 
New micro-hotel brand Arlo Hotels has a 50$ discount on rooms at its two New York properties--Arlo SoHo and Arlo NoMad--from now through Cyber Monday. The offer is available fro stays starting Dec. 1 to Sept. 4, 2018. Guests will also get a $50 credit towards food and beverage for a minimum two-night stay. 
Carillon Miami Wellness Resorthas two options: Get 35% off the best available rate plus a $150 spa credit, a $100 food and beverage credit, a waived resort fee and a breakfast for two if you book from Nov. 24 to Nov. 27. Rates starts at $486.25 per night with a two-night minimum.
The second option is also a 35% discount on the rate with a $350 spa credit and a $200 food and beverage credit. It also included a personal training session, an IV treatment, waived resort fee, and breakfast for two. Rates start at $551.25 per night with a four-night minimum.
The Embassy Row Hotel in Washington, D.C., a Destination Hotel, is offering 40% off the best available rate. Additional perks include complimentary valet parking, a daily $10 food and beverage credit per room, 10% off all food and beverage outlets at the hotel, unlimited phone calls, and complimentary access to the 24-hour Urban Fit Club Gym and Nucleus business center. It also includes complimentary access to the Underground Playground, which has air hockey, ping pong and foosball. There is also seasonal access to The Rooftop with yoga and meditation. The deal is available from Nov. 25 at 12 am EST through Nov. 27 at 11:59 pm EST with the promo code CYBER. The offer is valid on stays through August 2018.
Hotel Zelos in San Francisco, part of Viceroy Hotels, is offering up to a 40% discount on room rates and complimentary Wi-Fi from Nov. 27 to April 1. The Cyber Monday sale will run through Monday, Dec. 4. Hotel Zelos is a boutique hotel with large rooms and suites and patios. Room rates start in the mid-$200s.
At the Hotel Commonwealth in Boston, guests can stay for only $100 a night, a savings of 50%. The hotel is located near more than 100 colleges and universities. The sale starts Nov. 27 and ends after 100 minutes. It is valid for stays through March 7, and blackout dates do apply. To book, enter the code "Score100" or call 866.784.4000. 
The Resort at Paws Up in Montana has a 35% discount when you book a trip from Nov. 24 to Nov. 29. The offer is valid for stays from Jan. 2 to May 25, 2018. It is not, however, available for special events, holidays or other discounted stays. Paws Up is a luxury Montana ranch.
The Fontainebleau Miami Beach is discounting its room by 25% on stays for two nights or more. The deal includes complimentary breakfast for two. It is available through Nov. 27. 
The luxury Loews Hotels chain is giving guests a 20% discount if they book Nov. 27 to Nov. 29. The rates apply to properties in Hollywood, Philadelphia, New Orleans, Miami and more. Rates at properties such the one in Miami start at $299 and in Boston at $159.
Wyndham Hotel Group's Black Friday/Cyber Monday booking site is live: WHGCybersale.com. Guests can start booking up to 55% off hotel deals starting this Friday through Monday. The code is HCYB17. 
Kimpton Hotels and Restaurants is running a Cyber Monday / Giving Tuesday deal at 58 participating properties. Travelers can get up to 30% off best flexible rates when booking directly with Kimpton. The hotel chain will donate $5 a night to The Trevor Project and No Kid Hungry. The offer is valid for stays through March 31. Travelers much book by Nov. 28 and abide by a seven-day cancellation policy.
Marriott International has launched its Cyber Sale from now until Monday. more than 3,500 hotels across 20 brands through the USA, Caribbean, Latin America and Canada are participating. The sale features nightly rates at select properties as low as $89 on Marriott.com/cyber and member rates from $89 on SPG.com/cyber. The offer applies to stays from Dec. 7 to Jan. 15.
