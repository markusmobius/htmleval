Think Tanks Move to Fore on Energy
Jun 30, 2015 5:36 am ET 
 

Electricity-generating wind turbines in a corn field just outside Carlock, Ill.
Associated Press
In the world’s roiling energy markets, the think tank is having a day in the sun.
Antoine Halff, head of the oil industry and markets division for the International Energy Agency, the research and policy advisory arm of the OECD countries, is joining Columbia University’s Center on Global Energy Policy, an up-and-coming academic think tank, as a senior fellow and director of the center’s global oil markets research program.
