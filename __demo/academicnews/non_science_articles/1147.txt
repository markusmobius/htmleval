Carolina Moreno is a Latino Voices Editor for HuffPost, focusing on Latino issues, culture and entertainment. She's an NYU graduate with a degree in Journalism, Latin American Studies and French language. Her interests include Inter-American relations, human rights on both sides of the canal, race and diversity. She's in a loving but contradictory relationship with the world, humanity and what connects the two. To summarize: Joy junkie, cookie monster, LatAm enthusiast & diversity advocate.
