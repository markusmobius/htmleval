Planning a trip purely around eating great food sounds like a pretty great vacation to me. Where are travelers headed for a foodie vacation? According to Tripology, a division of USA TODAY Travel, New Orleans was the top destination city this week for a foodie vacation. With an average budget of $3,700 the culinary tours/food & wine traveler is planning now for a week of fine dining in late August to early September. Besides New Orleans, Manila, Naples, and New York were also hot foodie destination picks.
