Renee Fisher is the co-author of two award-winning books, Invisible No More: The Secret Lives of Women Over 50 and Saving the Best for Last: Creating Our Lives After 50. She speaks and leads workshops based on the books. Her blog is www.lifeintheboomerlane.com. She lives in Arlington, VA, with Now Husband Dan and Miracle the Cat. She would prefer a dog, but Now Husband Dan is a keeper.
