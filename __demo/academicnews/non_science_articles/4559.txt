Williams Partners Eyes Geismar Plant as Possible Source of Funds
Sept. 6, 2016 9:21 a.m. ET 
 
 Williams Partners LP said it is looking at ways to convert its olefins plant in Geismar, La., into another source of cash, including a possible sale or long-term fee-based services agreement.
Williams Partners, an affiliate of natural gas pipeline company Williams Cos., operates and has an 88.5% ownership interest in the Geismar plant.
