Tara Golshan Profile and Activity
Tara Golshan
Politics Reporter
 Tara Golshan covers Congress, elections, and just about anything in politics that needs explaining. 
 On her first day covering Congress she sat through the entire  18-hour Ways and Means Committee markup  of the House's Obamacare repeal bill. Since, she's written comprehensive explainers about congressional budgets, spending fights, the immigration debate, health care and tax reform. 
 She profiled Mark Meadows and keeps close tabs on the internal divisions within the Republican party. 
 Tara started at Vox in January 2016. She was born and raised in Northern California and went to university in Wisconsin.
