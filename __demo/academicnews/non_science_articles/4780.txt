Canada  Regal Lifestyle, Lumenpulse, AirBoss and more
Jun 18, 2015 9:55 am ET 
 
Revera Inc. and Health Care REIT Inc. have agreed to buy Regal Lifestyle Communities Inc. for about C$766 million. Shareholders of Regal, a retirement community company, will get C$12 per share in cash. Regal’s shares closed at C$9.44 on Wednesday.
Lumenpulse Inc. swung to an adjusted profit of C$800,000 in the fourth quarter of fiscal 2015, from a loss of C$1.3 million the year prior. The LED lighting maker recorded revenue of C$31 million for the quarter, a 14% increase from the year before. RBC started coverage of Lumenpulse at outperform on Tuesday.
AirBoss of America Corp. agreed to buy Immediate Response Technologies, LLC for about $37 million in cash. An extra amount of up to $25 million in cash and equity could be payable based on the company’s performance in the 60 months after the deal closes. TD raised the company’s rating to buy from hold, increasing its target price from $15.50 to $19.
