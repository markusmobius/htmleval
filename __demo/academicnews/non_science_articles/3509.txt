Gannett Appoints Matthew Barzun to Board of Directors
CONNECTTWEET 3 LINKEDINCOMMENTEMAILMORE
Gannett welcome Matthew Barzun, former U.S. ambassador to the United Kingdom, to its Board of Directors. Barzun has an impressive career in government and in providing strategic guidance for business model innovation. We are excited to welcome him to our board.
Barzun’s experience in digital transformation exceeds two decades, having joined CNET Networks in 1993 as its fourth employee. He held various management positions during his eleven years with that company, including Chief Strategy Officer. Barzun has also been a private investor in several internet start-ups and co-founded MedTrackAlert.
Regarding his governmental service, Barzun also served as U.S. ambassador to Sweden and National Finance Chair for former President Barack Obama’s re-election campaign. In addition, he volunteered for then-Senator Barack Obama’s 2008 presidential election campaign and led the effort to conduct supporter-driven small-dollar fundraisers.
Welcome Matthew Barzun to the Board of Directors and we look forward to his contributions and guidance as we move ahead.
