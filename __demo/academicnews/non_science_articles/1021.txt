Joy Behar Talks With Arianna Huffington And Rita Wilson About Huff/Post50
This morning on the "Joy Behar" show, Arianna Huffington and actress Rita Wilson talked to Behar about love and relationships after 50. 
Behar asked how Wilson, the Editor-at-Large of Huff/Post50, "keeps it hot with Tom Hanks."
On a more serious note, Huffington talks about how liberating it is to grow up and be free of self-doubt. 
"Life is not just about finding a guy," she said.
