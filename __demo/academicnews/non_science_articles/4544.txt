‘Café Müller’ and ‘The Rite of Spring’ Reviews: A Posthumous Homecoming
The double bill of pieces by Pina Bausch (1940-2009) now being performed at BAM repeats the program that first brought her work here in 1984.
 Sept. 18, 2017 5:22 p.m. ET 
 
 Brooklyn, N.Y. 
‘Café Müller” (1978) and “The Rite of Spring” (1975), the double bill of pieces by Pina Bausch (1940-2009) now being performed by Tanztheater Wuppertal Pina Bausch at BAM, repeats the program that first brought Bausch’s work here in 1984.
The bill runs an hour and 40 minutes, including intermission, far shorter than the single productions into which Bausch’s works evolved over the course of her extensive, internationally recognized career, programs that could last, with breaks, about three hours or more.
