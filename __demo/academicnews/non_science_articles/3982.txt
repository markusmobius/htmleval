Trump has found time to tweet about the "missing texts" — but not the Kentucky shooting

After news of Tuesday’s deadly school shooting in Kentucky broke, Canadian Prime Minister Justin Trudeau tweeted that he had spoken with Kentucky Gov. Matt Bevin and said that Canada’s “hearts go out” to all those affected by the tragedy. But more than 24 hours after the shooting, President Donald Trump has yet to comment publicly about the event. No tweets, no public statements issued by the White House, no press conference, nothing.
