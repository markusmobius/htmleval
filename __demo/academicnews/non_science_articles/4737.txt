Constellation Brands: This Trump Trade Has More Upside Ahead
Shares of Constellation Brands have suffered from President Donald Trump’s posturing toward Mexico, but the selloff looks overdone
 By 
 April 5, 2017 3:14 p.m. ET 
 
No one can stand in the way of America’s affection for Mexican beer—not even President Donald Trump.
Worries about Mr. Trump’s posturing toward Mexico have hurt Constellation Brands Inc., the largest U.S. distributor of popular Mexican beers including Corona. A tariff on Mexican imports would make its cheap beers that much more expensive, potentially hurting sales. Almost all of Constellation’s beer is imported from Mexico.
