Kristaps Porzingis shoves Marquese Chriss in Knicks-Suns scuffle
Knicks unicorn Kristaps Porzingis and Suns rookie Marquese Chriss got into a scuffle in the third quarter on Tuesday night.
After Porzingis blocked Chriss' shot, the two were running down the court and Chriss shoved Porzingis to the ground. KP then popped back up to give Chriss a good shove as Knicks players quickly ran over to stick up for their teammate and separate the two.
