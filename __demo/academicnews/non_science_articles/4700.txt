On the Right Track, CSX Is Now One Pricey Bet
Investors are optimistic about CSX’s new CEO Hunter Harrison, but he faces a high bar following the stock’s sharp rally 
 By 
 April 18, 2017 3:18 p.m. ET 
 
Who said you can’t put a price on reputation? In the case of CSX Corp., it appears to be a cool $9 billion.
That is nearly how much the railroad’s market value has surged since January, when the idea of industry maverick Hunter Harrison taking a leadership role was floated. Best known for his turnaround prowess through cutting costs and improving operations, the 72-year-old industry veteran was named CSX’s chief executive last month....
