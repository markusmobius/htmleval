When you’re watching football, sometimes nature calls. That’s why you bring Febreze. This year the air freshener, which is made by Procter & Gamble, is making its first Super Bowl ad appearance.
Narrated by actress Kathryn Hahn (Step Brothers, Transparent), the 30-second commercial is an ode to the halftime bathroom break. Agency Grey New York created the spot, which fittingly will air in the second quarter.
