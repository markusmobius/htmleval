Deripaska’s EN+ to Raise $1.5 Billion in IPO
Updated Oct. 5, 2017 2:51 p.m. ET 
 
LONDON—Russian metals tycoon Oleg Deripaska’s EN+ Group PLC on Thursday said it expects to raise about $1.5 billion through an initial public offering in November, the biggest listing of a Russian company since the annexation of Crimea in 2014.
