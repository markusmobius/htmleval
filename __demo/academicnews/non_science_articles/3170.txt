USA TODAY NETWORK Selected for GoPro's Pilot Program
CONNECTTWEETLINKEDINCOMMENTEMAILMORE
USA TODAY NETWORK is among a small group to receive GoPro's new Fusion camera as part of a newly launched pilot program.
In April, GoPro began taking applications from content professionals seeking to be among the first to use GoPro's new camera as part of a program to produce inspiring content that demonstrates the creative potential of the Fusion. This first wave of pilot partners represents well-known brands, agencies and talented content professionals who are actively pursuing the use of spherical and 360-degree cameras in their production, including USA TODAY NETWORK.
USA TODAY NETWORK is one of 10 selected out of 20,000 applicants.
To experience USA TODAY NETWORK's existing VR and 360 content, visit www.usatoday.com/vrtuallythere.
