Breeders' Cup 2017: How to watch Saturday's races, odds, more
The $6 million race is on.
Saturday at the 2017 Breeders' Cup features nine championship races at California's Del Mar Racetrack, including the $6 million Classic. Arrogate, last year's champion, is running at 2-1 odds while Gun Runner is the morning-line favorite at 9-5.
Here's everything you need to know for Saturday’s action:
How to watch
First 8 races: 1:30 - 8:00 p.m. ET, NBC Sports Network
Breeders' Cup Classic: 8-9 pm. ET, NBC.
