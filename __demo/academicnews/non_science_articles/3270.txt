2015 college football TV schedule
College football season is entering its bowl season and climaxes with the second College Football Playoff championship game on Jan. 11, 2016 in Glendale, Ariz.
Below are games scheduled for national networks, with listed times p.m. Eastern unless otherwise noted. Dates and times are subject to change. Most Saturday games on ABC, CBS, FOX, FS1 and ESPN's family of networks post-Week 3 are announced between two weeks and six days in advance, so game times and TV information will be added throughout the season.
