USA TODAY Honors Princess Diana with Extensive View of Her Legacy
CONNECTTWEET 35 LINKEDINEMAILMORE
For the 20-year anniversary of the death of Princess Diana, USA TODAY has put together an extensive digest of coverage to honor her memory and keep her legacy alive.
 Pulling from resources across the country, USA TODAY Life-led coverage includes content ranging from first person accounts from reporters who covered her death to unique interviews with key folks in her life to provide a portrait of the Princess we may never have seen before. Other themes include Princess Diana’s fashion legacy, the conspiracies that still surround her death, her charitable work and how her sons are carrying on that legacy, how her death changed the royal family, and many more.
 “Princess Diana’s death was something that rocked not just Europe but the US as well. To this day, people are still talking about where they were when they heard she had passed.” said Alison Maxwell, USA TODAY Life managing editor. “It was important for us to capture the essence of her legacy for the 20th anniversary of her death to provide our readers with a holistic snapshot of who she was as we celebrate her life.”
 The complete list of coverage can be found at diana.usatoday.com.
