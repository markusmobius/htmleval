How the Democrats See the GOP Tax Plan
Sens. Amy Klobuchar and Mark Warner say they are for tax reform. But not the way the Republicans are going about it.
 Nov. 19, 2017 10:11 p.m. ET 
 
With Republicans in control of all three branches of the federal government, Paul Beckett, Washington bureau chief of The Wall Street Journal, asked two Democratic senators—Amy Klobuchar of Minnesota and Mark Warner of Virginia—what their party’s message will be heading into the 2018 elections.
