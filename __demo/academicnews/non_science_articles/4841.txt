An Adviser Dips a Toe Into Real Estate
A property fund is one element in a suggested portfolio for moderate-risk investors
 July 9, 2012 
 
After years of staying away from real-estate investments, Iowa financial adviser Kevin Timmerman started buying some for clients in April.
Despite the sharp gains in real-estate investments since the trough in early 2009, "we still think there's some upside," says Mr. Timmerman, president of Steele Capital Management Inc. in Dubuque.
