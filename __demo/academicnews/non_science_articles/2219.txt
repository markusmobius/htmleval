Kathryn Minor, Geoffrey Jones
Credit Pepper Nix

Kathryn Marchand Minor and Geoffrey Gardner Jones were married Jan. 13 at Stein Eriksen Lodge in Park City, Utah. The Rev. William Hamill, a Roman Catholic priest and friend of the family, officiated.
Mrs. Jones, 39, is the vice president of executive affairs and operations at EverFi, an education technology company in Washington. She graduated from Southern Methodist University and received a law degree from Tulane.
She is the daughter of Paul S. Minor Sr. of New Orleans and the late Sylvia Fleming Minor. The bride’s father, who is retired, was a lawyer in private practice in both New Orleans and Biloxi, Miss.
Mr. Jones, 47, works in Washington as the chief financial officer and the senior vice president of business development at Monarch Global Strategies, a strategic consulting firm. The groom’s father, James R. Jones, is the chairman of the firm, which advises companies on doing business in Mexico and Latin America. The groom graduated from Stanford, from which he also received an M.B.A.
He is a son of Mr. Jones of Washington, and the late Olivia Barclay Jones.
The groom’s father was a congressman from Oklahoma (1973-87), president of the American Stock Exchange (1989-93) and ambassador to Mexico (1993-97).
The couple were set up by a mutual friend and met in November 2008 on a blind date in Washington.
