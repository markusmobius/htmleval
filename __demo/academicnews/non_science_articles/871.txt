Mondays With Marlo - Holiday Tips and Trimmings
No matter how many times we enter the holiday season, the story remains the same - we're usually overwhelmed. From cooking to decorating to shopping for presents, each season seems to present a different set of obstacles. The thing to remember, though, is that you're not alone! We've been so thankful to have a lot of talented and resourceful holiday experts on the show over the years. To help you navigate this holiday season, we've compiled a bundle of amazing holiday tips from some of our favorite guests. See how you can save money on shopping and flights with advice from super savers Suze Orman, Trae Bodge, and frugal traveller Seth Kugel. Learn how to save money on decorations by using your food, with tips from Sandra Lee and Limor Suss. And you won't believe how Kelly Ripa deals with three energetic children, how Gail Saltz manages family drama, and how Trisha Yearwood manages her husband's sense of tradition. There's plenty of great info to go around - presents for everyone!
