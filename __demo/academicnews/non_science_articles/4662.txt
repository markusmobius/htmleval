Stone Energy Rises 6.6%, Hornbeck Adds 4.9%
Updated July 15, 2009 12:01 a.m. ET 
 
NEW YORK -- A pause in the recent slide of oil prices helped spur broad buying in energy stocks Tuesday, leading small-caps higher.
Though crude oil closed down 17 cents at $59.52 a barrel, the commodity's decline was slowed down Tuesday. Traders say the impact of weak demand is becoming priced into the market after improved demand expectations had led to a false surge in oil earlier this year.
