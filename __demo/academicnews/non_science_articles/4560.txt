Some Buying Is Reported
In Stock Mutual Funds
By 
 Updated Sept. 2, 1998 10:25 p.m. ET 
 
Stock mutual-fund investors appeared to calm down Tuesday, with fund groups reporting a mixture of buying and selling as the market rebounded. Fund managers were in a similar mood: some were nibbling at stocks, others were selling, but few appeared to be doing heavy trading.
