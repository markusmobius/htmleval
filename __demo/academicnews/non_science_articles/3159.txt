 Rapper LL Cool J was born James Todd Smith in Queens, NY, on Jan. 14, 1968. Grammy-nominated nine times and twice a winner, the artist is known for hits like 'Mama Said Knock You Out,' 'Doin' It,' 'I Need Love,' 'Around The Way Girl' and 'Hey Lover.' His last album was entitled 'Authentic.' NONE
