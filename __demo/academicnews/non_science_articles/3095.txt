 Julia Roberts, Owen Wilson and Jacob Tremblay star in this exclusive scene from 'Wonder.' Lionsgate

Movie and TV adaptations translated into big book sales in 2017. Wonder, R.J. Palacio 2012 kids’ novel about a boy with severe facial irregularities, is USA TODAY’s top-selling book of the year, thanks to the film adaptation starring Jacob Tremblay and Julia Roberts. The book was in the top 10 for 16 weeks in 2017.
The Handmaid’s Tale, The Shack, Big Little Lies and Stephen King’s It also got lifts from screen adaptations.
